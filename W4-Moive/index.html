Little did he know that when he contacted that man he was then put through tourture to mutate his genes to give him super healing. He would then fight his way out of that place and after finding out how ugly he is, he wants to go back to his cancer filled body. He then fights trough every one in his way to find the guy that did it to him, only to find no cure.
